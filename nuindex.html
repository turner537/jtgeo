jtGeo Ltd. is an independent UK-based company specialising in geospatial consultancy alongside highly bespoke software development. Further services include first-class support and training.

With expertise in both land and marine domains, jtGeo delivers significant mission-critical projects where accuracy and rigour are essential. Recent projects include the design and development of software for the SnakeGrid coordinate system, development of advanced FME plug-ins and workspaces, complex algorithms for deriving oil and gas sector business intelligence from marine AIS and the impact of metocean data on marine geophysical surveying.

Clients include UCL-Business, Network Rail, HS2 and Seisintel.
